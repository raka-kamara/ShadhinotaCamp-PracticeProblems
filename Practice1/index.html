Simple Omelette Recipe


An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal this classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetable, or meats

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetable,, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a flutter texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Heat the pan: Place a no-stick frying pan over medium heat and butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Heat the pan: Place a no-stick frying pan over medium heat and butter or oil.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Heat the pan: Place a no-stick frying pan over medium heat and butter or oil.
  5. Fold and serve As the omelette Heat the pan: Place a no-stick frying pan over medium heat and butter or oil.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
